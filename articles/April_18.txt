==Events==
* [[796]] &ndash; King [[Æthelred I of Northumbria]] is murdered in [[Coria (Corbridge)|Corbridge]] by a group led by his [[ealdormen]], Ealdred and Wada. The ''patrician'' [[Osbald of Northumbria|Osbald]] is crowned, but abdicates within 27 days.  
*[[1025]] &ndash; [[Bolesław I Chrobry|Bolesław Chrobry]] is crowned in [[Gniezno]], becoming the first [[List of Polish monarchs|King]] of Poland.
*[[1506]] &ndash; The [[cornerstone]] of the current [[St. Peter's Basilica]] is laid.
*[[1518]] &ndash; [[Bona Sforza]] is crowned as [[queen consort]] of Poland.
*[[1521]] &ndash; Trial of [[Martin Luther]] begins its second day during the assembly of the [[Diet of Worms]]. He refuses to recant his [[Lutheranism|teachings]] despite the risk of excommunication.
*[[1689]] &ndash; [[Boston]]ians rise up [[1689 Boston revolt|in rebellion]] against Sir [[Edmund Andros]].
*[[1738]] &ndash; ''[[Real Academia de la Historia]]'' ("Royal Academy of History") is founded in [[Madrid]].
*[[1775]] &ndash; [[American Revolution]]: The [[Battles of Lexington and Concord|British advancement by sea]] begins; [[Paul Revere]] and other riders warn the countryside of the troop movements.
*[[1797]] &ndash; The [[Battle of Neuwied (1797)|Battle of Neuwied]]: French victory against the Austrians.
*[[1831]] &ndash; The [[University of Alabama]] is founded.
*[[1847]] &ndash; American victory at the [[battle of Cerro Gordo]] opens the way for invasion of Mexico.
*[[1857]] &ndash; "[[The Spirits Book]]" by [[Allan Kardec]] is published, marking the birth of [[Spiritualism]] in France.
*[[1864]] &ndash; [[Battle of Dybbøl]]: A [[Kingdom of Prussia|Prussian]]-[[Austrian Empire|Austrian]] army defeats Denmark and gains control of [[Duchy of Schleswig|Schleswig]]. Denmark surrenders the province in the following peace settlement.
*[[1897]] &ndash; The [[Greco-Turkish War (1897)|Greco-Turkish War]] is declared between Greece and the [[Ottoman Empire]].
*[[1899]] &ndash; The [[St. Andrew's Ambulance Association]] is granted a [[Royal Charter]] by [[Queen Victoria]].
*[[1902]] &ndash; The 7.5 [[Moment magnitude scale|Mw]] [[1902 Guatemala earthquake|Guatemala earthquake]] shakes [[Guatemala]] with a maximum [[Mercalli intensity scale|Mercalli intensity]] of VIII (''Severe''), killing between 800–2,000.
*[[1906]] &ndash; [[1906 San Francisco earthquake|An earthquake]] and fire destroy much of [[San Francisco, California]].
*[[1909]] &ndash; [[Joan of Arc]] is beatified in Rome.
*[[1912]] &ndash; The [[Cunard Line|Cunard]] liner {{RMS|Carpathia|}} brings 705 survivors from the {{RMS|Titanic|}} to New York City.
*[[1915]] &ndash; French pilot [[Roland Garros (aviator)|Roland Garros]] is shot down and glides to a landing on the German side of the lines during [[World War I]].
*[[1923]] &ndash; [[Yankee Stadium (1923)|Yankee Stadium]]: "The House that [[Babe Ruth|Ruth]] Built" opens.
*[[1936]] &ndash; The first [[Champions Day]] is celebrated in [[Detroit|Detroit, Michigan]].
*[[1942]] &ndash; [[World War II]]: The [[Doolittle Raid]] on Japan: Tokyo, [[Yokohama]], [[Kobe]] and [[Nagoya]] are bombed.
*  1942   &ndash; [[Pierre Laval]] becomes [[Prime Minister]] of [[Vichy France]].
*[[1943]] &ndash; World War II: [[Operation Vengeance]], Admiral [[Isoroku Yamamoto]] is killed when his aircraft is shot down by U.S. fighters over [[Bougainville Island]].
*[[1945]] &ndash; Over 1,000 [[bomber]]s attack the small island of [[Heligoland]], Germany.
*[[1946]] &ndash; The [[International Court of Justice]] holds its inaugural meeting in [[The Hague]], Netherlands.
*[[1949]] &ndash; Ireland leaves the [[Commonwealth of Nations|British Commonwealth]] and becomes the [[Republic of Ireland]].
*1949 &ndash; The keel for the aircraft carrier {{USS|United States|CVA-58|6}} is laid down at [[Newport News Drydock and Shipbuilding]]. However, construction is canceled five days later, resulting in the [[Revolt of the Admirals]].
*[[1954]] &ndash; [[Gamal Abdel Nasser]] seizes power in [[Egypt]].
*[[1955]] &ndash; Twenty-nine nations meet at [[Bandung]], [[Indonesia]], for the first [[Asian-African Conference]].
*[[1980]] &ndash; The [[Republic of Zimbabwe]] (formerly [[Rhodesia]]) comes into being, with [[Canaan Banana]] as the country's first President. The [[Zimbabwean dollar]] replaces the [[Rhodesian dollar]] as the official currency.
*[[1983]] &ndash; A [[suicide bomber]] [[1983 United States embassy bombing|destroys the United States embassy]] in [[Beirut]], [[Lebanon]], killing 63 people.
*[[1988]] &ndash; The United States launches [[Operation Praying Mantis]] against [[Iran]]ian naval forces in the largest naval battle since World War II.
*[[1992]] &ndash; General [[Abdul Rashid Dostum]] revolts against President [[Mohammad Najibullah]] of the [[Democratic Republic of Afghanistan]] and allies with [[Ahmad Shah Massoud]] to capture [[Kabul]].
*[[1996]] &ndash; In Lebanon, at least 106 civilians are [[1996 shelling of Qana|killed]] when the [[Israel Defense Forces]] shell the [[United Nations]] compound at Quana where more than 800 civilians had taken refuge.
* 2007 &ndash; A [[18 April 2007 Baghdad bombings|series of bombings]], two of them being [[suicide bomber|suicides]], occur in [[Baghdad]], killing 198 and injuring 251.
*[[2013]] &ndash; A [[18 April 2013 Baghdad bombing|suicide bombing]] in a [[Baghdad]] cafe kills 27 people and injures another 65.
