==Events==
*[[215 BC]] &ndash; [[Temple of Venus Erycina|A temple]] is built on the [[Capitoline Hill]] dedicated to [[Venus Erycina]] to commemorate the [[Roman Empire|Roman]] defeat at [[Battle of Lake Trasimene|Lake Trasimene]].
* [[599]] &ndash; Maya king [[Scroll Serpent|Uneh Chan]] of [[Calakmul]] attacks rival city-state [[Palenque]] in southern Mexico, defeating queen [[Yohl Ik'nal]] and sacking the city.
* [[711]] &ndash; [[Dagobert III]] is crowned [[King of the Franks]].
*[[1014]] &ndash; [[Battle of Clontarf]]: High King of Ireland [[Brian Boru]] defeats [[Viking]] invaders, but is killed in battle.
*[[1016]] &ndash; [[Edmund Ironside]] succeeds his father [[Æthelred the Unready]] as king of England.
*[[1343]] &ndash; [[St. George's Night Uprising]] commences in the [[Danish Estonia#Duchy of Estonia|Duchy of Estonia]].
*[[1348]] &ndash; The founding of the [[Order of the Garter]] by King [[Edward III of England|Edward III]] is announced on [[Saint George|St. George's Day]].
*[[1516]] &ndash; The Bayerische [[Reinheitsgebot]] (regarding the ingredients of beer) is signed in [[Ingolstadt]].
*[[1521]] &ndash; [[Battle of Villalar]]: [[Charles V, Holy Roman Emperor|King Charles I of Spain]] defeats the [[Revolt of the Comuneros|Comuneros]].
*[[1635]] &ndash; The first public school in the United States, [[Boston Latin School]], is founded in [[Boston]].
*[[1655]] &ndash; The [[Siege of Santo Domingo (1655)|Siege of Santo Domingo]] begins during the [[Anglo-Spanish War (1654–60)|Anglo-Spanish War]], and fails seven days later.
*[[1660]] &ndash; [[Treaty of Oliva]] is established between Sweden and Poland.
*[[1661]] &ndash; King [[Charles II of England]], Scotland and Ireland is crowned in [[Westminster Abbey]].
*[[1815]] &ndash; [[Second Serbian Uprising|The Second Serbian Uprising]]: A second phase of the national revolution of the Serbs against the [[Ottoman Empire]], erupts shortly after the annexation of the country to the Ottoman Empire.
*[[1879]] &ndash; Fire burns down the second main building and dome of the [[University of Notre Dame]], which prompts the construction of the third, and current, [[Main Building (University of Notre Dame)|Main Building]] with its golden dome.
*[[1914]] &ndash; First [[baseball]] game at [[History of Wrigley Field|Wrigley Field]], then known as Weeghman Park, in Chicago. 
*[[1918]] &ndash; World War I: The [[Royal Navy|British Royal Navy]] makes a [[Zeebrugge Raid|raid]] in an attempt to neutralise the Belgian [[port of Bruges-Zeebrugge]].
*[[1920]] &ndash; The [[Grand National Assembly of Turkey]] (TBMM) is founded in [[Ankara]]. The assembly denounces the government of [[Sultan]] [[Mehmed VI]] and announces the preparation of a [[Turkish Constitution of 1921|temporary constitution]].
*[[1927]] &ndash; [[Cardiff City F.C.|Cardiff City]] defeat [[Arsenal F.C.|Arsenal]] in the [[1927 FA Cup Final|FA Cup Final]], the only time it has been won by a team not based in England.
*[[1935]] &ndash; The [[Polish Constitution of 1935]] is adopted.
*[[1940]] &ndash; The [[Rhythm Club fire]] at a dance hall in [[Natchez, Mississippi]], kills 198 people.
*[[1941]] &ndash; [[World War II]]: The Greek government and [[George II of Greece|King George II]] evacuate [[Athens]] before the invading [[Wehrmacht]].
*[[1942]] &ndash; World War II: [[Baedeker Blitz]] &ndash; German bombers hit [[Exeter]], [[Bath, Somerset|Bath]] and [[York]] in retaliation for the British raid on [[Lübeck]].
*[[1945]] &ndash; World War II: [[Adolf Hitler]]'s designated successor [[Hermann Göring]] sends him [[Göring Telegram|a telegram]] asking permission to take leadership of the [[Third Reich]], which causes Hitler to replace him with [[Joseph Goebbels]] and [[Karl Dönitz]].
*[[1946]] &ndash; [[Manuel Roxas]] is elected the last President of the [[Commonwealth of the Philippines]].
*[[1948]] &ndash; [[United Nations Security Council Resolution 48]] is adopted.
*[[1949]] &ndash; [[Chinese Civil War]]: Establishment of the [[People's Liberation Army Navy]].
*[[1951]] &ndash; American journalist [[William N. Oatis]] is arrested for espionage by the [[Communist]] government of [[Czechoslovakia]].
*[[1955]] &ndash; The [[Canadian Labour Congress]] is formed by the merger of the [[Trades and Labour Congress of Canada]] and the [[Canadian Congress of Labour]].
*[[1961]] &ndash; [[Algiers putsch of 1961|Algiers putsch]] by French generals.
*[[1967]] &ndash; [[Soviet space program]]: [[Soyuz 1]] (Russian: Союз 1, Union 1) a manned spaceflight carrying [[cosmonaut]] Colonel [[Vladimir Komarov]] is launched into orbit.
*[[1968]] &ndash; [[Vietnam War]]: Student protesters at [[Columbia University]] in New York City [[Columbia University protests of 1968|take over administration buildings and shut down the university]].
*[[1971]] &ndash; [[Bangladesh Liberation War]]: The Pakistan Army and [[Razakar (Pakistan)|Razakars]] [[Jathibhanga massacre|massacre]] approximately 3,000 Hindu emigrants in the Jathibhanga area of [[East Pakistan]] (now [[Bangladesh]]).
*[[1985]] &ndash; [[Coca-Cola]] changes its formula and releases [[New Coke]]. The response is overwhelmingly negative, and the original formula is back on the market in less than three months.
*[[1990]] &ndash; [[Namibia]] becomes the 160th member of the [[United Nations]] and the 50th member of the [[Commonwealth of Nations]].
*[[1993]] &ndash; [[Eritrea]]ns vote overwhelmingly for independence from [[Ethiopia]] in a [[United Nations]]-monitored referendum.
*  1993   &ndash; [[Sri Lanka]]n politician [[Lalith Athulathmudali]] is [[Assassination of Lalith Athulathmudali|assassinated]] while addressing a gathering, approximately four weeks ahead of the Provincial Council elections for the [[Western Province, Sri Lanka|Western Province]].
*[[2013]] &ndash; At least 28 are dead and more than 70 are injured as [[2013 Hawija clashes|violence breaks]] out in [[Hawija]], [[Iraq]].
