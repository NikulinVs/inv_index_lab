'''Aa''' is the name of a large number of small European rivers. Aa originated from an Indo-European word meaning water, and it can be seen in the German ''Ach'' or ''Aach'' or the North Germanic ''A'' or ''Aa''.<!--<ref name=EA>{{cite encyclopedia |editor-first=Patricia |editor-last=Bayer |encyclopedia=Encyclopedia Americana |title=A |edition=First |year=2000  |publisher=Grolier Incorporated |volume=I A-Anjou |location=Danbury, CT |isbn=0-7172-0133-3 |pages=1 }}</ref>-->
