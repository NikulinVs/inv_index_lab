==Events==
*[[670]]  &ndash; The second [[Shia]] Imam [[Hassan ibn Ali]] passes away after being poisoned
*[[1513]] &ndash; Spanish explorer [[Juan Ponce de León]] first sights land in what is now the [[United States]] state of [[Florida]].
*[[1755]] &ndash; [[Commodore (Royal Navy)|Commodore]] [[William James (naval commander)|William James]] captures the [[Maratha]] fortress of [[Suvarnadurg]] on west coast of India.
*[[1792]] &ndash; The [[Coinage Act of 1792|Coinage Act]] is passed establishing the [[United States Mint]].
*[[1800]] &ndash; [[Ludwig van Beethoven]] leads the premiere of his [[Symphony No. 1 (Beethoven)|First Symphony]] in [[Vienna]].
*[[1801]] &ndash; [[Napoleonic Wars]]: [[Battle of Copenhagen (1801)|Battle of Copenhagen]]: The British capture the Danish fleet.
*[[1851]] &ndash; [[Mongkut|Rama IV]] is crowned [[Monarchy of Thailand|King]] of [[Thailand]].
*[[1863]] &ndash; [[Richmond bread riot]]: Food shortages incite hundreds of angry women to riot in [[Richmond, Virginia]], and demand that the [[Confederate States of America|Confederate]] government release emergency supplies.
*[[1865]] &ndash; [[American Civil War]]: Defeat at the [[Third Battle of Petersburg]] forces the [[Army of Northern Virginia]] and the Confederate government to abandon [[Richmond, Virginia]].
*[[1885]] &ndash; Canadian [[Cree]] warriors [[Frog Lake Massacre|attack]] the village of [[Frog Lake, Alberta|Frog Lake]], killing nine.
*[[1900]] &ndash; The [[United States Congress]] passes the [[Foraker Act]], giving [[Puerto Rico]] limited [[self-rule]].
*[[1902]] &ndash; [[Dmitry Sipyagin]], [[Ministry of Internal Affairs (Russia)|Minister of Interior]] of the [[Russian Empire]], is assassinated in the [[Marie Palace]], [[St Petersburg]].
*  1902   &ndash; "Electric Theatre", the first full-time [[movie theater]] in the United States, opens in [[Los Angeles]].
*[[1911]] &ndash; The [[Australian Bureau of Statistics]] conducts the country's first [[Census in Australia|national census]].
*[[1912]] &ndash; The ill-fated {{RMS|Titanic}} begins [[sea trial]]s.
*[[1917]] &ndash; [[World War I]]: United States President [[Woodrow Wilson]] asks the [[United States Congress|U.S. Congress]] for a [[declaration of war]] on [[German Empire|Germany]].
*[[1921]] &ndash; The [[Autonomous Government of Khorasan]], a military government encompassing the modern state of [[Iran]], is established.
*[[1930]] &ndash; After the mysterious death of Empress [[Zewditu]], [[Haile Selassie]] is proclaimed emperor of [[Ethiopia]].
*[[1956]] &ndash; ''[[As the World Turns]]'' and ''[[The Edge of Night]]'' premiere on [[CBS-TV]]. The two soaps become the first daytime dramas to debut in the 30-minute format.
*[[1972]] &ndash; Actor [[Charlie Chaplin]] returns to the United States for the first time since being labeled a [[communism|communist]] during the [[Second Red Scare|Red Scare]] in the early 1950s.
*[[1973]] &ndash; Launch of the [[LexisNexis]] computerized legal research service.
*  1973 &ndash; The [[Liberal Movement (Australia)|Liberal Movement]] breaks away from the [[Liberal and Country League]] in [[South Australia]].
*[[1975]] &ndash; [[Vietnam War]]: Thousands of civilian [[refugee]]s flee from [[Quảng Ngãi Province]] in front of advancing [[North Vietnam]]ese troops.
*[[1979]] &ndash; A Soviet bio-warfare laboratory at Sverdlovsk [[Sverdlovsk anthrax leak|accidentally releases airborne anthrax spores]], killing 66 plus an unknown amount of livestock. 
*[[1980]] &ndash; United States President [[Jimmy Carter]] signs the [[Crude Oil]] [[Windfall Profits Tax]] Act in an effort to help the U.S. economy rebound.
*[[1982]] &ndash; [[Falklands War]]: [[1982 invasion of the Falkland Islands|Argentina invades the Falkland Islands]].
*[[1986]] &ndash; [[Alabama]] governor [[George Wallace]], a former [[segregationist]], best known for the "[[Stand in the Schoolhouse Door]]", announces that he will not seek a fifth four-year term and will retire from public life upon the end of his term in January 1987.
*[[1989]] &ndash; [[Soviet Union|Soviet]] leader [[Mikhail Gorbachev]] arrives in [[Havana]], [[Cuba]], to meet with [[Fidel Castro]] in an attempt to mend strained relations.
*[[1991]] &ndash; [[Rita Johnston]] becomes the first female [[Premier (Canada)|Premier]] of a [[province (Canada)|Canadian province]] when she succeeds [[William Vander Zalm]] (who had resigned) as [[Premier of British Columbia]].
*[[1992]] &ndash; In [[New York (state)|New York]], [[American Mafia|Mafia]] boss [[John Gotti]] is convicted of murder and [[racketeering]] and is later [[Life imprisonment|sentenced to life]] in prison.
*  1992 &ndash; 42 civilians [[Bijeljina massacre|were massacred]] in the town of [[Bijeljina]].
*[[2002]] &ndash; [[Israel]]i forces surround the [[Church of the Nativity]] in [[Bethlehem]] into which armed [[Palestinians]] had retreated; a siege ensues.
*[[2004]] &ndash; [[Islamism|Islamist]] [[terrorism|terrorists]] involved in the [[11 March 2004 Madrid attacks]] attempt to bomb the Spanish high-speed train [[AVE]] near [[Madrid]]; the attack is thwarted.
*[[2006]] &ndash; Over 60 [[tornado]]es [[April 2, 2006 tornado outbreak|break out]] in the United States; [[Tennessee]] is hardest hit with 29 people killed.
*[[2011]] &ndash; India won the [[2011 Cricket World Cup]], defeating [[Sri Lanka]] by 6 wickets in the [[2011 Cricket World Cup Final|final]] in Mumbai, thus becoming the first country to win the [[Cricket World Cup]] final on home soil.
*[[2012]] &ndash; A [[Oikos University shooting|mass shooting at Oikos University in Oakland, California]], leaves seven people dead and three injured.
*[[2014]] &ndash; A [[2014 Fort Hood shooting|spree shooting]] occurs at the [[Fort Hood]] Army Base near the town of [[Killeen, Texas]], with four dead, including the gunman, and 16 others injured.
*[[2015]] &ndash; Gunmen [[Garissa University College attack|attack]] [[Garissa University College]] in [[Kenya]], killing at least 148 people and wounding 79 others.
